Trash Notices		






There are multiple reasons why your trash or recycling may have been skipped, see the chart below for potential remedies to the violation in question.Reason For Notice:Remedy:Over-sized BarrelTake your bags out of the receptacle and place them to the curb with your next pickup.Purchase a new trash receptacle that is less than or equal to 50 gallons in volume.Barrel or Bag Too HeavyRemove the heavier items from the barrel/ bag and place them in a separate bag to make them more manageable.Non Recyclables/ Trash in Recycling BinRemove the trash from your recycling bin.Medical/ Hazardous WasteRemove these items and register with us for a Hazardous Household Waste Disposal Event.Leaves in Plastic BagsRemove the leaves from the plastic bag and put them in a bio-degradable bag.Mattress/ Box Spring Not CoveredWrap mattresses and box springs before placing them to the curb.Mattress bags are available for free at 200 Henry Johnson Blvd, 1 Richard J Conners Blvd and 24 Eagle Street.For additional information please feel free to give us a call at (518) 427-7480.For the full e-code regarding trash disposal in Albany: click here.
