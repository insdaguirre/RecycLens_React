Garbage Collection		






The purpose of Albany’s waste collection and recycling program is to collect normal weekly generated household trash and recycling from residential properties with 4 or less units. Excessive quantities of trash may be subject to a bill and fine. Excessive amounts of trash is defined as anything not manageable by 1 person, unable to be loaded into a collection vehicle and takes laborers more than 4 trips between the pile and the vehicle. Trash should be placed curbside, prior to 12:01a.m. on the scheduled collection day but not before 4:30 pm of the prior day.NOTE: Trash cans are not permitted to be stored in public view. All garbage should be containerized using heavy-duty plastic bags and/or metal or plastic containers. Metal or plastic garbage containers shall have a maximum volume of 50 gallons and be fitted with a lid. If you wish to dispose of a broken or damaged garbage container please label "CONTAINER BROKEN, PLEASE DISPOSE. " BULK TRASH (e.g. household furniture, mattresses and toilets) may also be placed at the curb in limited quantities.Please call the Department of General Services at (518) 434-CITY (2489) at least 24 hours prior to your collection day to inform us of these items. NOTE: Please call during normal business hours (M-F 7am to 5pm).List of Acceptable Bulky Trash:CouchesLoveseatsSofa BedsArm Chairs/ReclinersTables/ChairsBedsFutonsDressersMattresses & Box SpringsWood Bed FramesArmoiresShelvingTV StandsPorcelain Tubs/Sinks/ToiletsPianos/OrgansMisc. FurnitureBULKY RECYCLABLES OR WHITE GOODS (e.g. Air conditioners, refrigerators, freezers, stoves, hot water tanks and tires) can also be placed curbside. Please call the Department of General Services at (518) 427-7480 at least 24 hours prior to your collection day to inform us of these items. NYS LAW: Doors must be removed from refrigerators and freezers prior to being put curbside. NOTE: Please call during normal business hours (M-F 7am to 5pm).List of Acceptable White Goods:Air conditionerDehumidifierDishwasherClothes DryerFreezer (NYS Law: Doors must be removed)Refrigerator (NYS Law: Doors must be removed)FurnaceHot Water TankMicrowave OvensLawn Mower (All fluids must be removed)Snow Blower (All fluids must be removed)StoveTiresWashing Machine (NYS Law: Doors must be removed)RadiatorCast Iron TubCast Iron SinkHOUSEHOLD HAZARDOUS WASTE & ELECTRONICS Hazardous waste and electronics are NOT accepted curbside, however, the City of Albany sponsors numerous FREE Household Hazardous Waste Drop Off Events each year.
