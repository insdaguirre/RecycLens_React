Household Hazardous Waste 








The City of Albany offers 9 free Household Hazardous Waste (HHW) collection events annually as a way for Albany residents to dispose of HHWin an easy, safe and responsible way.


LOCATION OF EVENTSCity of Albany Landfill525 Rapp RoadAlbany, New York 12205Time of Events: 4:00pm to 7:00pm 








WHO IS ELIGIBLE? DGS provides this service to City of Albany residents only. Small quantity generators (SQG) - Businesses that generate less than 32 gallons of hazardous waste in a month may participate but will be charged a fee.NOTE: SQGs must register by contacting the Department of General Services here. PRE-REGISTER FOR DROP-OFF EVENTS: To participate in one of the collection events, you must pre-register: Register by  phone by calling (518) 434-2489 (Monday through Friday 8:00 am to 5:00 pm)Or register here.  Phone registration is closed the Friday before each event, and online registration is closed the Tuesday before each event. (Registration could close earlier if maximum capacity is reached for the event) It is our collective responsibility to dispose of HHW responsibly. HHW should never be placed curbside. If you have any questions regarding the Household Hazardous Waste & Electronics Recycling program, please contact the Department of General Services at 518-427-7480 or email DGS here.

ELECTRONICS


In addition to the year-round collection of electronics at the Department of General Services facility (One Richard J Conners Blvd.), electronics are also accepted at all Household Hazardous Waste DROP OFF events. A list of acceptable electronics is listed below: • Computers, Scanners, Printers, Fax Machines• Ink & Toner Cartridges• Monitors, Keyboards & Mice• Portable Digital Devices• Game Consoles• Wires and Cords• Music Players, Stereos & Speakers• TVs, VCRs, Video & DVD Devices• Cell Phones & Chargers
