Paper Shredding and Book Recycling Events


Albany Department of General Services sponsors two (2) FREE Paper Shredding events each year. Albany residents can bring documents to be shredded, and they will be shredded on-site. DGS also collects hard and soft-cover books for recycling. Books are NOT redistributed/reused. We would like to inform residents that there will be only one shredding event this year.The event will be held in front of the Washington Park Lake House.Please enter through the Madison Ave entrance.   2026 Dates To be determined.   Please try to transport papers to be shredded in boxes, reusable totes, or paper bags. They will be returned once your paper has been dumped. Residents will have the opportunity to watch their paper being shredded.
