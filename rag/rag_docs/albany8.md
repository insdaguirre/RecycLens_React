Compost Facility


City of Albany compost is pure yard waste compost. Only brush, branches, leaves, and grass clippings collected from Albany residents or deposited directly at the Compost Facility are used to make City of Albany Compost. Wood chips are also produced at the Compost Facility from organic waste collected throughout the city. Included in this is several thousand Christmas trees collected curbside annually. 








The compost Facility is located at: Department of General Services One Richard J. Conners Blvd. Albany, New York 12204Phone (518) 427-7480 The Compost Facility is closed for the season as ofFriday, November 14, 2025  Hours of Operation: Monday-Friday 8:00 am to 12:00 pm; 12:30 pm-2:30 pm(Closed 12:00 pm – 12:30 pm for lunch)  


City residents can obtain compost and mulch free of charge during regular business hours at the compost facility. Residents must check in at the booth and show proof of residency before obtaining compost or mulch.  There is a maximum limit of 30 gallons or (6) five-gallon buckets of each item per resident daily.  FREE COMPOST AND MULCH IS FOR RESIDENTIAL USE FOR CITY RESIDENTS ONLY. Compost and mulch are no longer available at the dead end of Erie Blvd.


For commercial or nonprofit interests, we have a convenient process in place. You may obtain a permit and be billed monthly for the quantities purchased. For a copy of the permit application, please call (518) 427-7480. This clear procedure is designed to facilitate your application and ensure a hassle-free experience.  Residents, Non - Residents & Commercial Generators: Yard waste is accepted at the Compost Facility at a cost of $10.00 per cubic yard. The attendant on duty reserves the right not to accept any material deemed contaminated, excessive volumes, oversized items, or otherwise inappropriate for the compost processing equipment. Note: Any yard waste containing stones or rocks will be refused.Please call (518) 427-7480 for a copy of the permit application.The following items are available for purchase during regular business hours:• Screened Compost $15.00 cyd• Un-Screened Compost $10.00 cyd (when available)• Mulch\Woodchips $15.00 cyd • Topsoil $20.00 cyd• Historic Cobblestones $2.00 each Please be informed that all transactions must be paid for with cash or check at the Department of General Services Main Office before the loading or unloading the vehicle. We would like to emphasize that credit cards are not accepted. Under no circumstances will payments be accepted at the compost facility itself. This is to avoid any confusion and ensure a smooth transaction process. Commercial or nonprofit interests may obtain a permit and be billed monthly for the quantities purchased. For a copy of the permit application, please call (518) 427-7480.
