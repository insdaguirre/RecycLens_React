Landfill Drop Off Program


 









Residents Only - No Businesses
MOTOR OIL AND ANTIFREEZE (Landfill Drop-off)5 gallons per person per visit or take to any oil change service station 
	 
SINGLE STREAM (NO SORT) RECYCLABLE MATERIALSCheck the Recycling Basics or Broome County Recycling Guide if you are unsure about what materials are accepted
	 
BULK MIXED RIGID PLASTICS RECYCLING(This is a special plastic recycling program that is separate from the regular recycling program materials)  Plastic bucket (NO metal handles)
	Plastic milk/soda crate
	Plastic laundry baskets and totes
	Plastic lawn furniture
	Plastic drums and garbage cans
	Plastic toys and pet carriers
	Plastic coolers
	Plastic shelving
	Plastic flower pots and trays (no soil)
	PET blister pack
	Automotive plastic (bumpers, pick-up truck bed liners, grills, hub caps)Material NOT accepted as part of the bulk mixed rigid plastic drop-off at the landfill are:
	PVC pipe/tubing, vinyl siding
	Glass, metal, wood, paper, clothes, Styrofoam
	Poly-coated paper
	Flexible water hoses, hazardous material or medical waste
	Narrow neck liquid containers; small plastic containers (yogurt cups & bottles)
	Oil/chemical containers.
For More Information Call Division of Solid Waste Management 607.778.2250
