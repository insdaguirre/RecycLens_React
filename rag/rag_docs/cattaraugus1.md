Refuse Division Public Education - Tire Recycling







 
CATTARAUGUS COUNTY DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS PRESENTS
Tire Recycling
February 2025 Edition | Refuse Division Fun Facts
 
DID YOU KNOW? 


In New York State, an estimated 18-20 million waste tires are generated each year. 


From 2021-2023 Cattaraugus County disposed of 564 tons of waste tires!


An average car tire weighs 22 pounds. 


Agricultural and industrial tires can weigh over 300 pounds!


NYS requires a $2.50 recycling fee (per tire) on most retail new tire sales within the State. 


 
CATTARAUGUS COUNTY FEES
CLEAN, DRY TIRES ACCEPTED!


Car tire (up to 21”) $5.00


Car tire (up to 21” on rim) $15.00


All other tires: Scaled at the Salamanca Transfer Station


Large truck tires (19.5”-24.5”)


Agricultural & industrial tires (15”-21”)


Oversized (>24.5”) 




NOTE: Minimum scale fee of $20


Additional fees for dismounting


LIMIT OF FOUR (4) TIRES PER PERSON, PER DAY at ALL TRANSFER STATIONS EXCEPT SALAMANCA
