Medication Disposal		






You can safely dispose of unused prescription and over-the-counter medicines 24 hours a day at the Cayuga County Public Safety Building: 7445 County House Road Auburn, NY 13021. To learn what items are accepted, please review the Dispose of Excess Medication (PDF).
