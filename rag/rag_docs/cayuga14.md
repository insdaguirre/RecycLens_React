Electronics Waste Program		






Event InformationThe 2025 event was held on October 18th and resulted in the collection of over 45,000 pounds of electronic materials to be reused or recycled. If you still have electronics that you need to get rid of, please visit our "What To Do I Do With..?" page for more information.General Event DetailsThe Cayuga County Department of Planning and Economic Development typically holds a collection event in the month of April or May and then another in the month of September or October. Information regarding time and location is distributed about a month before the event takes place. Registration is required. 



 
 
 





Typically Accepted ItemsTV's & Monitors Computers & AccessoriesData Center Equipment Mobile DevicesOffice Equipment Lab & Medical EquipmentEntertaining EquipmentAnd more
