Polystyrene Foam (Styrofoam)		






Polystyrene foam, or Styrofoam, is a synthetic polymer, most often used in packaging materials, that can have significant harmful effects on the environment if handled improperly. The material takes hundreds of years to break down in a landfill but is easily recycled. Polystyrene Foam (Styrofoam) Recycling Options:Thermal Foams, Inc.Thermal Foams is a polystyrene foam manufacturer with locations throughout New York State including just north of Syracuse. They offer a recycling drop off service to the community at no charge. Everything that is collected is recycled into their own products. We recommend that you call the facility at the number listed below prior to dropping materials off!!Accepted Materials - Clean, white polystyrene foam only (NO food containers or packaging peanuts)Location - 6173 South Bay Rd, Cicero, NY 13039Hours - Monday-Friday 6:30am-3:00pmContact Information - (315) 699-4969For more information on recycling with Thermal Foams, follow this LINK.
