Freon		






Freon OverviewFreon represents a variety of chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs), which contain elements such as carbon and fluorine as well as other gases. Freons are colorless, odorless, non-flammable, non-corrosive gases/liquids that are hazardous to the environment. Some common appliances containing freon include:Refrigerators Freezer UnitsWater CoolersDehumidifiersAir Conditioning UnitsPicking up Old AppliancesRiester's Appliances, Lowes and Home Depot sell appliances and it is recommended that you have them pick up your old appliance when they deliver your new one.Other Appliances Recycling ProgramsAuburn Transfer StationThe City of Auburn provides Freon appliance recycling services for City and County residents for a fee. Residents with a Transfer Station Permit ($20 for City residents & $120 for out-of-City residents) may drop off appliances at the Auburn Transfer Station. Pick-ups are available within the City of Auburn but must be pre-arranged.
