            We also accept the following free for Recycling: 
Batteries 
Wet Cell (Automotive, Lawn Mower, Motorcycle) 
All Household and Rechargeable  
Lithium Ion terminals must be taped with clear tape 
Scrap Metal 
Any scrap metal and appliances not containing Freon 
Used Motor Oil and Antifreeze 
Used motor oil and antifreeze, Maximum 5 gallons per resident per day 
Electronics and Fluorescent Bulbs 
All Electronics no limit, Fluorescent bulbs Limit 2 per resident per day 
Paint 
Chenango County, in a partnership with PaintCare, is able to accept all  
paints, stains, and shellacs in their original container for recycling.  Check out  
https://www.paintcare.org/products/ for a complete list of acceptable 
materials 
 
Items Not Accepted For Recycling 
(can be accepted as garbage) 
**NOT A COMPLETE LIST** 
Ceramics, Light Bulbs, Crystal, Drinking Glasses, Household 
Dishes, Pyrex, Plastic Lined Bags, Mirror Glass, Plastic Film, 
Styrofoam, Window Glass, Soda or Beer Boxes, Waxy or Plastic 
Coated Boxes, Diapers, Potato Chip Bags, Candy Wrappers, 
Rubber Bands, CD’s, Hoses, Latex Gloves, Utensils, PVC Pipe, Used 
Tissues/Paper Towels, Prescription/Non-prescription Drugs 
 
Residential Permits are Free and availble at all facilities. 
 
ALL LOADS MUST BE COVERED PER NEW YORK STATE LAW 
UNCOVERED LOADS WILL BE REJECTED
