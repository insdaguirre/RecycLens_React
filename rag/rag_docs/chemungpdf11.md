Asbestos 
Asbestos MUST be placed in appropriate bags or lined containers 
and taken to the Pharsalia Landfill with 24 hours notice given to 
the scale operator.  Bags are available for purchase at all sites. 
Asbestos Bags………………………………………….……………..……$1.00/Bag 
(Purchase of bag only) 
Disposal of Asbestos……………………….….….…..$4.00/Bag or $85/Ton

  Source Separated Recycling 
All Clean Recycling……..……………………….…………………….FREE 
(Recycling MUST be separated by type) 
 
Plastics  
All plastic MUST be clean and sorted. Look for the symbol below 
         #1 PETE Bottles and Tubs  
 
         #2 HDPE Must be separated by Colored and Natural 
 
         #5 PP  Jugs/Tubs and Lids 
All caps are garbage 
Paper 
All Paper MUST be Clean and Dry  
NO soiled, waxy or plastic coated paper or boxes 
Newsprint – All Newsprint (if it comes with the newspaper it can be 
recycled with the newspaper, also Phone books with covers removed) 
Corrugated Cardboard/Boxboard – must have all packaging  
removed, please break down all boxes, Brown Paper Bags 
Junk Mail -  Ads, Magazines, Catalogs, Envelopes, Coupons, 
Greeting Cards, Computer/Copy/Fax paper, Shredded Paper 
Glass 
MUST be CLEAN bottle or jar with lids Removed 
Glass Containers – Food or Beverage Jar or Bottle Separated by 
Color (Clear, Brown,Green/Blue) 
Metal Food Cans 
All Metal Food/Beverage Cans MUST be Clean 
Tin Cans – Any Steel/Tin food Cans or metal caps from jars 
(dogfood/soup/sauce) NO Aerosal Cans 
Aluminum – Any Aluminum food/beverage cans/CLEAN Foil/Trays                         
(Cat food/beer/soda/Tea) NO Aerosal Cans 
Leaves, Yard Waste and Brush 
NO PLASTIC, MASONRY OR LUMBER 
leaves, pine needles/cones, wood chips, weeds, remains of garden 
plants, old hay and brush under 4” in diameter
