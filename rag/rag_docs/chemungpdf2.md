Cardboard Recycling 
FLATTEN CARDBOARD — Keep LOOSE — do NOT TIE bundles together!! 
Accepted  
 
• CORRUGATED CARDBOARD  
• Kraft Paper 
Not Accepted  
• Plastic Bags  
• Food-contaminated Cardboard 
• Plastic shipping pillows (recyclable with plastic film at Participating retail stores) 
• Packing Peanuts/Styrofoam 
• Paper of any kind
