Mixed Paper Recycling 
Accepted (All Paper should be Clean and Dry)  
 
• Junk mail (incl. ads, Magazines/Catalogs, envelopes, coupons)  
• Computer/Copy/Fax, Wrapping Paper (not paper foil/metallic)  
• Writing/Ledger, Greeting Cards, Shredded Paper   
 NOT Accepted  
 
• Wax or Plastic-coated Paper 
• Boxes, Tissues, Napkins 
• Wet or Food-contaminated Paper Towels 
• Newsprint (Must be recycled in separate bin) 
• Plastic wrappers 
 
 
 
**IF YOU ARE NOT SURE ABOUT AN ITEM PLEASE ASK! **
