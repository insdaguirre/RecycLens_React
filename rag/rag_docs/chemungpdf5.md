Plastic Recycling 
   
Accepted (Must Be Clean and Empty) 
 
• All #1 PETE Tubs and Bottles can be recycled with #1 PETE 
• #2 HDPE Must be separated by Natural (Uncolored) and Colored 
• #5 PP tubs and trays and lids  
 
Not Accepted 
 
• STYROFOAM  
• Soiled Containers 
• Garden Hoses 
• Furniture 
• Oversized plastic 
• Plastic toys 
• Screw Caps 
**Just because there is a triangle doesn’t make it recyclable** 
**IF YOU ARE NOT SURE ABOUT AN ITEM PLEASE ASK! **
