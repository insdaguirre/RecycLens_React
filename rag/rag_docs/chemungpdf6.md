Tin Cans 
 
Accepted 
• All metal food cans and lids 
**A magnet WILL stick 
 
 
 
 
NOT Accepted  
 
• Soiled Aluminum Foil/Trays 
• Scrap Metal/Pipes (must be recycled with scrap metal) 
• Any metal building materials (screws/nails/clean paint cans, must be recycled with scrap metal) 
• Aerosol Cans (may be recycled with scrap metal) 
 
 
 
 
**IF YOU ARE NOT SURE ABOUT AN ITEM PLEASE ASK! **
