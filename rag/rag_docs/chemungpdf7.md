Aluminum Recycling 
 
Accepted 
• CLEAN Aluminum Foil/Trays 
• Aluminum Beverage Cans 
• Aluminum Cat Food Cans 
**A magnet will NOT stick 
 
 
NOT Accepted 
 
• Soiled Aluminum Foil/Trays 
• Scrap Metal/Pipes (must be recycled with scrap metal) 
• Aluminum Rails/Doors/Sheeting/Roofing (must be recycled with scrap metal) 
• Aerosol Cans (may be recycled with scrap metal) 
 
 
 
**IF YOU ARE NOT SURE ABOUT AN ITEM PLEASE ASK! **
