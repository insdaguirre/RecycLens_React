Glass Recycling 
  
 Must be a CLEAN bottle or jar with lids removed 
 
 
Accepted 
• Beverage Bottles 
• Food Jars 
• Must be sorted by Color  
(Clear, Green/Blue, Brown) 
NOT Accepted 
• Household Dishes/Ovenware/Pyrex/Ceramics/Porcelain 
• Window Glass 
• Light Bulbs (Fluorescent Bulbs may be recycled separately)   
• Mirrors 
• Crystal Glass 
STOP
