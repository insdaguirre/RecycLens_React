Use of Solid Waste Facilities and Residential Landfill PermitIn order to use any Chenango County Solid Waste Management Facility, all vehicles must have a residential permit sticker present on their driver's door window.The residential permit is free to all Chenango County residents and are vehicle specific, if you have a sticker from an older vehicle they cannot be used on a new vehicle.
