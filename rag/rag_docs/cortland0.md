Cortland County Landfill Policies





Only wastes generated in Cortland County are permitted.
No liquid, hazardous or burning wastes will be accepted.
No prohibited items allowed as posted at the Cortland County Landfill (see list below).
No smoking.
One vehicle allowed on the open face for dumping at a time, unless otherwise directed by a Cortland County Landfill employee.
Safety vests must be worn if unloading on the open face.
Must remain in vehicle unless removing tarp in staging area or unloading.
Follow all other posted policies prominently displayed at the facility and/or the Cortland County website.
Follow all directives of Cortland County Landfill employees while using the facility.
