 WHAT IS HAZARDOUS WASTE?
 

Hazardous waste is any 
					product that is toxic in nature and should NOT be disposed 
					of as everyday trash.

It requires special handling, 
					processing, and disposal.

Some of these materials may 
					be recycled but are not part of the routine recycling 
					program.
