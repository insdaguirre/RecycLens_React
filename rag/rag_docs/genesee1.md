Household Hazardous Waste






What is Household Hazardous Waste?
It is any waste produced in the home, which is flammable, toxic, corrosive or reactive. It includes certain paint products, pesticides and herbicides, hobby chemicals, automotive fluids and cleaning products.
Who Can Participate?
Any resident of Genesee, Livingston or Wyoming Counties.
How Can I Participate?
Materials are accepted by appointment at GLOW’s annual collections. The 2025 collection was held in Batavia. Contact the GLOW office for copies of the final reports for these collections.  The 2026 collection will be held in Livingston County, with dates to be determined.  
 
Residents without Internet access are encouraged to call (585)815-7906 or (585)344-2580 extension 5463 with questions regarding Household Hazardous Waste or GLOW Collection Events.  
 
Acceptable Materials:

Propane Canisters & Tanks
Auto & Marine Batteries
Oil Filters & Antifreeze
Miscellaneous Vehicle Fluids
Pesticides & Insecticides
Household Cleaning Products
Polish & Waxes
Solvents & Corrosives
Resins & Adhesives
Pool & Photo Chemicals
Spray paint
Driveway Sealer
Fluorescent Light Bulbs & Tubes
Full or Partially Full Aerosol Cans
Tires - $5.00 each for passenger/light truck tires and $10.00 each for truck tires

 
Unacceptable Materials

Motor Oil
Foam insulation tanks (known as froth paks)
Explosives & Ammunition
PCB's & Asbestos
Infectious/Biological Waste
Pathogenic Waste
Pressure Sensitive Reactive Material
Needles and Syringes
Pharmaceuticals
Tractor tires
Oil or latex paint
