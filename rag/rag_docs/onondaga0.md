Rock Cut Road map




Rock Cut Road






Trash & Recycling








5808 Rock Cut Road
Jamesville, NY 13078









								(315) 453-2866							








[email protected] 







Hours
ONONDAGA COUNTY RESIDENTS ONLY (all household materials/projects, small and large loads, delivered by the resident – not a hauler or contractor). This facility serves residents across Onondaga County; it is not limited to Jamesville residents. No appointment needed:

Monday – Friday: 3:30 p.m. to 6 p.m.
Saturday: 8 a.m. to noon
Residents must unload their own materials. OCRRA cannot provide assistance.

Holiday Closings: New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day.
BUSINESSES ONLY (including landlords, contractors and hired haulers)

Monday – Friday
Hand-unloading: 6:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.
Dump vehicles 6:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
