Whats happening with recycling?







The Department of Sustainability & Solid Waste Management is updating its Strategic Plan for the next 5 years. The 6 Key Focus Areas for the Department's 2026-2031 Strategic Plan are:Equal and Adequate AccessFunding to Advance Department GoalsLegislation Aligned with County's Plan for Solid waste ManagementEducation & CommunicationsDepartment's Role Post-Landfill ClosureInfrastructure PrioritiesTo read the full draft plan please click here.
