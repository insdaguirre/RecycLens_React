Mattresses & Furniture		






Sunken Couch? Extra Armchair? You Should Do the Right ThingWe all have an old recliner or unused table in the garage or basement. And it feels good to de-clutter your space, right? So, find out where you can easily donate that unwanted furniture without kicking it to the curb. There are convenient spots to drop it off all over Ontario County. Be sure to call ahead and ask about any potential fees or limitations in what they’ll take. All items for donation should be in good condition.Find out where you can drop off old furniture.Have a mattress that can't be donated? Bring it to one of our mattress recycling events.
