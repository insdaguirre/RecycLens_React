Scrap Metal Recycling		






Per 6 NYCRR Part 371.1(c)(7), Small Quantity Generators of Scrap Metal (e.g. School Districts, Businesses, Farms, and Municipalities) are required to complete and submit the following form to the weigh master at each corresponding Orange County operated transfer station.Residents bringing in scrap metal material generated in their own household are not required to file a C7 form.
