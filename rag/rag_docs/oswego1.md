Solid Waste
        



 



Michael Lutestanski II Director of Solid Waste Programs2801 NYS-481Fulton, NY 13069The Department of Solid Waste is responsible for providing recycling and solid waste services to residents, businesses and commercial waste haulers in the County.
All  transfer stations will be CLOSED on Thursday, December 25, and Thursday, January 1in observance of Christmas Day and New Year's Day“Fix-It Clinic” at the Fulton YMCA          A free “Fix-It Clinic” offers repair services to community members in an effort to reduce environmental waste and save space in our landfills.The clinic runs from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. on Saturday, Jan. 10 at the Fulton YMCA, 715 W. Broadway, Fulton.Residents are encouraged to bring their small appliances, furniture, clothing, jewelry, toys and electronics to the event where other citizens will share their expertise and provide needed repairs.NOTICEBeginning Thursday, July 3, Hastings transfer station will close its main building for maintenance.During this process, trash and recycling will be dropped off in the recycling building. Residents are asked to follow signage and staff instructions to help make this a smooth transition.
