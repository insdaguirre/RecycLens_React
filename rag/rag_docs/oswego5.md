Household Hazardous Waste
        



 



Residential Household Hazardous WasteThe household hazardous waste facility  is open May to September for residents(Wednesdays/Saturdays only). The facility is located at the Bristol Hill Solid Waste Facility at 3125 NYS-3, Fulton.Household Hazardous Waste CLOSED until Saturday May 2, 2026                                     Items accepted from residents:

adhesives
aerosols
antifreeze
auto batteries
ballasts 
brake fluid
cements
degreasers
disinfectants
dry gas
flea products
fluorescent bulbs
gasoline
hobby chemicals
household cleaners
insect repellents
lacquers
lighter fluids
lubricants
mercury
latex paint - maximum 5 containers (up to 25 gallons)
oil-based paint 
paint removers
paint thinners
pesticides
pool chemicals
rat poisons
rug cleaners
solvents
turpentine
varnish
weed killers
wood stains

Business-Generated Hazardous WasteOur facility is available to conditionally exempt small quantity generators. Complete the small generator form to receive a quote.
