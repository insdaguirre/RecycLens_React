Home Departments / Offices S-Y Solid Waste Muncipal Drop Off Centers
                        
 

Muncipal Drop Off Centers



Municipal Drop-Off Center Information SheetPlease link to the following for information on each drop off location:


	











Cherry Valley


Located on NYS Route 166 north of village next to the town hall
Open Saturdays 8am - 11am
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from any person regardless of residency
Hometown Hauling of Worcester accepts the garbage and charges by the bag and accepts other specialty items and charges by the unit
Call town highway superintendent at 607-264-8491 for more information








Cooperstown

Otsego County Northern Transfer Station

Located on the east side of Highway 28/80 approximately one mile north of the Village of Cooperstown
21 front load units are available for commercial haulers and the public.
One county employee is there to assist residents and look after the site during opened hours.

Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday 7AM - 12PMSaturday - 8AM - 12PMFore more information visit our homepage. 







Decatur


Located on county highway 37 behind the Decatur town barn
Open Saturdays 7 am - 2 pm
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from town of Decatur residents only (free permit from town clerk required) at no charge
Scrap metal items are accepted from any resident at no charge for deposit in a separate metal container
Call town clerk at 607-397-7437 for more information

 







Hartwick


Open every 24/7
Located between town hall and town barn in hamlet
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Call town clerk at 607-293-8123 for more information








Maryland


Located on Tannery Road (4/10 mile from intersection with NYS Route 7 near western edge of Schenevus)
Open Mondays and Saturdays 7am - 1pm
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from town of Maryland residents only ($10 permit from town clerk required)
Budget Refuse of Maryland provides all garbage services
Specialty items, such as white goods, scrap metal, tires, furniture, brushand construction/demolition debris, are accepted from town of Maryland residents only
Prior payment to the town clerk is required for specialty items, and she will issue receipt that must be shown when items are taken for disposal
Tire collection day June 3-5th (4 tires per household per day)
Roll off one weekend in July, August and September
Call town clerk at 607-638-1924 for more information








Morris


Located on Card Road (about ¼ mile from intersection with NYS Route 23) on the eastern edge of the village
Open Thursdays 8am - 12pm and Saturdays 8am - 2pm
Recyclables accepted from town and village of Morris residents
Bagged garbage accepted from town and village of Morris residents only at no charge, with free permit from the transfer station attendant. 
Small brush accepted from town and village of Morris residents
White goods are accepted from town and village of Morris residents on Thursdays or Saturdays with a charge of $15 for each item that requires freon removal (which is done on-site)
Specialty items, such as mattresses and furniture, are accepted about twice per year from town and village of Morris residents only. Mattresses/Box Springs: $25/unit
Call town clerk at 607-263-5944 for more information








Oneonta

Otsego County Southern Transfer Station

Located at 75 Silas Lane, adjacent to Exit 13 of Interstate 88 near the western edge of the City of Oneonta
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Solid waste, including specialty items such as tires and white goods are accepted from any county resident
Fees are charged by Casella for solid waste deliveries based upon number of bags, tonnage or the number of units of specialty items. Check current rate schedule for fees. 

Hours of Operation:Monday - Friday 7:00AM - 3:00PMSaturdays - 8:00AM - 12:00NOON








Richfield


Located on Elm Street Extension near the town/village line
Open Saturdays 7AM - 12PM
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from any county resident
Hometown Hauling of Worcester accepts the garbage and charges by the bag
Scrap metal items are accepted from any county resident at no charge
Brush is accepted from village of Richfield Springs residents only
Call town clerk at 315-858-8809 for more information








Pittsfield


Located on Mumbalo Road, Off County Rte 49
Open Saturday 9-3
Recyclables are accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from Town residents ONLY (no fee)
Scrap Metal accepted from Town Residents ONLY 








Springfield


Located on NYS Route 80 about 2 mile north of the intersection with U.S. Route 20
Open Saturdays 8am - 12pm
Recyclables accepted from any county resident
Bagged garbage accepted from town resident
Casella accepts the garbage and charges by the bag
Scrap metal items are accepted from any county resident at no charge for deposit in a separate metal container
Call town supervisor at 315-858-0108 for more information








Westford


Located on Strong Hill Road, north of the hamlet
Hours are Tuesday and Saturday from 6am-2pm
Trash is accepted only from town of Westford residents at no charge
Scrap metal is accepted from anyone with no fee
Clean lumber is also accepted from town of Westford residents only









Worcester


Located at the Town Barn, Off South Hill Road
Recycling Hours:  Saturday 7AM - 11AM
Trash Accepted - Saturdays 7AM - 11AM  Fee: $3/bag
Scrap metal is accepted on Saturdays 7AM - 11AM
