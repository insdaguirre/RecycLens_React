                Household Hazardous Waste Facility
              




The Household Hazardous Waste Facility (HHW) is a drop-off collection facility that accepts household hazardous waste materials from Rockland County residents and small businesses that qualify for CESQG status. View a map and directions or call (845) 364-2444 for more information.
