							Medication Takeback
						


Medication Disposal
Keep medications in their original containers.
Controlled Substances
Controlled and non-controlled substance medications can be brought to the Rockland County Sheriff’s Office, located at 55 New Hempstead Rd., New City 24 hours a day.
Non-Controlled Substances
Non-controlled substance medications can be brought to the Rockland Household Hazardous Waste Facility at 35 Firemen's Memorial Drive on the dates listed below.
