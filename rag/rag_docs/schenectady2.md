 Schenectady County Household Hazardous Waste Collection Program

 

Keep your environment healthy and safe by getting rid of unwanted and left over hazardous chemical items. 
Household hazardous chemicals are any products in the home which are labeled as flammable, toxic, corrosive, or reactive. To ensure proper disposal, contact the Schenectady County Household Hazardous Waste Collection Program. 


This program is for Schenectady county residents only
A composting Facility and Resident Recycling Center permit is required to register. 
Register online at www.schenectadycounty.com/hhw or call 518-372-1622 x264
