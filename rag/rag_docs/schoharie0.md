Recycling Locations

Home Departments Solid Waste & Recycling Recycling Locations




The Schoharie County Transfer Station offers free recycling services to residents Monday through Saturday. Additionally, residents can access municipal recycling locations within their towns at no cost. Some locations also accept white goods (i.e. kitchen appliances) and tires. For residents of Middleburgh, the Town Barn provides convenient disposal options for paint and yard waste. Please contact the Solid Waste & Recycling Coordinator at 518-295-8447 for more information. * - Location accepts Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) from residents. Other Recycling Options:
