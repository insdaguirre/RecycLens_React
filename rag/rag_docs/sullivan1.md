County Transfer Stations / Environmental Facilities









The Sullivan County Department of Solid Waste & Recycling operates the Sullivan County Transfer Station & Recycling Center in Monticello, as well as five residential dropoff stations throughout the county. Each facility is open specific days and hours as noted on the chart below. Our facilities accept many types of solid waste and recyclables. Please contact the facility directly for information on specific materials and charges.

Fees: Fees are charged for the disposal of all solid waste. There are no fees for most recyclables. Please refer to complete fee information.
