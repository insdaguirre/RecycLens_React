                Curbside Recycling Guides            

Curbside Recycling Guide are available to understand what types of cardboard, plastics, glass, metal, and cartons can be recycled in Westchester:

Our Recycle Right Brochure (en español) describing County recycling procedures is available for home printing.
Our informative Pocket Guide is another valuable resources explaining how to recycle common materials.
Video and printable presentations about our Recycle Right program are available on our Facebook page. Stay up-to-date, by liking our page.
Questions about recycling specific items can be directed to our Recycling Helpline at (914) 813-5425 or 211.
Recycling information is also available from Earth 911.

The list of acceptable materials is determined partly by what the County's solid waste and recycling facilities are equipped to sort and manage. Your cooperation is greatly appreciated.
