If you collection day falls on or after these holidays, your collection day will be delayed by one day.  Residents with a regular collection day on Friday will be collected on a Saturday on these weeks.View Holiday ScheduleService Guides & Instructions Trash   Service & Collection InformationWM provides a 96 gallon cart for weekly trash collection. To view your specific service day, please click here, enter your address and select "Verify”. Container Setout InstructionsPlace container on the street with wheels against the curb by 6 a.m. on collection days and remove same day. Keep container at least three (3) feet from parked cars, mailboxes, and other obstacles that may prevent WM team members from picking it up. Recycling cart must be out with trash for pick up.  How to Prepare Your Materials All trash should be bagged. Do not overfill your container – the lid must close tightly. Any bags outside cart must have the Official Overflow Sticker.  What Belongs in Your Container  Acceptable items include standard household trash like food waste, chip bags, paper towels, plastic bags, and textiles (donate if lightly used).Non-acceptable items include construction waste, auto parts, tires, household hazardous waste (paint, oil, household cleaners), medical or electronic waste.
